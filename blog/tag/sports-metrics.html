  • Why Sports Metrics? Building

    Tue 18 March 2025

    I've been tracking my training and ultimate frisbee games for some time now, and I recently decided to turn that into something of an adjacent project for attempting to develop metrics to quantify my performance. The initial motivation stems from watching a lot (a lot!) more Premier League soccer and starting to learn more about how high level soccer teams track their players to quantify statistics. Some metrics are out of my league, but some are much more attainable. For example, I can implement metrics such as: Total Distance covered, Sprint Distance, Power, and Top Speed.
